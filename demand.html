    Demand Curves-Movements and Shifts

    • Introduction to the determinants of demand
      • A change only in the price of a good or service will result in a movement along the demand Curve.
      • A change in anything else will result in a shift of the demand curve.
      • The determinants of demand are those factors that will shift (move) a demand curve.
    • The Determinants of Demand are SPICE-T
      • Subsitute Goods' prices changing
      • Population changing
      • Income of consumers (as a whole) changing
      • Complimentary goods' as prices changing
      • Expectation of consumers changing
      • Tastes of consumers changing
