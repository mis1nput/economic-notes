Unemployment

  • Adult Population
    • Anyone over the age of 16 who has a job orr is seeking a job.
  • Labor Force
    • Those in the adult population who are working and those who are not working.
  • Employed V. Unemployed
  • Unemployment Rate (U3 and U6)
    • U3=(# unemployed / labor force) *100
    • U6 includeds discouraged workers & marginally-attached workers

Types of Unemployment

  • Frictional
    • Those who quit or are fired or are seeking their first job
  • Cyclical
    • Those who are laid off due to recessions-downward portion of the buisness cycle
  • Structural
    • Those who lack the skills to get hired or whose jobs are eliminated due to systemic Changes
